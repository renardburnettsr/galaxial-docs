How Galaxial works

Galaxial remembers what matters across every conversation — so you never have to re-explain yourself.

What makes it different

Most AI tools forget everything the moment you close the tab. Ask them the same question tomorrow and you're starting from scratch.

Galaxial remembers. Tell it you're planning a trip to Japan, working on a small business idea, or trying to learn guitar — and next time you come back, it already knows.

How to start a conversation

The more specific you are upfront, the better Galaxial can help. You don't need to be technical — just talk to it like you'd talk to a friend who happens to know a lot.

Less helpful

"Hey, can you help me with my thing?"

Much better

"Hey, I'm working on a small catering business called Lena's Kitchen. I want help writing a menu description for my Instagram page."

Tips for getting the most from it

📌
Name your projects

If you're working on something ongoing — a business, a trip, a creative project — give it a name and use it consistently. Galaxial will keep it all organised.

✏️
Correct it when it gets something wrong

Just say "actually that changed — it's now X." Galaxial will update and move on. No need to start over.

🔖
Tell it what's still open

If you're in the middle of a decision, say so. "I haven't decided yet" or "this is still a work in progress" helps Galaxial treat it as ongoing rather than settled.

💬
Just talk to it naturally

You don't need special commands. Galaxial figures out what's important from how you talk. The more you use it, the better it gets at understanding you.

What it won't do

  • It won't invent memories or fill gaps with guesses
  • It won't mix up your information with someone else's
  • It won't drift off topic when you're mid-project

Your conversations are private. Only you can see your history and memory. Galaxial never shares your information with other users.
